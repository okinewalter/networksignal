About

The purpose of this project is to collect and synthesise large amounts of data to interpret the network signal strength of telecommunication networks and produce a web application that can easily be accessed to make readily available the current signal strengths (of MTN, Vodafone, and Airtel Tigo) on the Accra Institute of Technology Kokomlemle Campus.Â  The outcome of this project can inform the students and staff about the regions on campus with better network signals, which network is better under what conditions, and which network is better at what time. The outcome of this study can inform the telecommunication companies of areas on campus where they can improve their service and conditions. The study's findings can also help a new student decide which telecommunications network to use based on the locations he or she will be visiting. Enjoy sustainable network use with our system.
