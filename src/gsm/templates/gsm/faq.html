The system works by periodically collecting data on each of the telecommunication networks signal readings at vantage areas on the AIT KCC campus and then performing average calculations on data collected. The system then make predictions based on the averaging results.

The accuracy of the data for the system is dependent on the frequency of data collection. Our team collects the network signal data of each of the telcos three (3) times (begininig – midweek – weekend) every week and feed the system with the data for a more current and reliable prediction to be made?

We currently have data on the following locations on the KCC Campus: Ground Floor, First Floor, Second Floor, Third Floor, Fourth Floor, Fifth floor and the Sixth Floor.
